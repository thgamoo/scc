# MAT (Mathematics) Course Catalog

## MAT 118 - Mathematical Thinking
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** Satisfaction of entry skill in mathematics
- **Description:** Logic, sets, combinatorics, probability, statistics, graph theory. For non-science majors.

## MAT 119 - Foundations for Precalculus
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** Level 1 on math placement exam
- **Description:** Arithmetic, algebraic expressions, equations, inequalities, functions, graphing.

## MAT 122 - Overview of Calculus with Applications
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** C or higher in MAT 123 or level 5+ on math placement exam
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 151, MAT 125, 126, 131, 132
- **Description:** One-semester survey of calculus for non-STEM majors.

## MAT 123 - Precalculus
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** Level 3+ on math placement exam
- **Description:** Polynomial, rational, exponential, logarithmic, trigonometric functions; analytic geometry.

## MAT 125 - Calculus A
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** C or higher in MAT 123 or level 5+ on math placement exam
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 151 or MAT 131 or 141
- **Description:** Limits, continuity, derivatives, applications of differentiation.

## MAT 126 - Calculus B
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** C or higher in MAT 125 or AMS 151 or level 7+ on placement
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with MAT 127, 132, 142
- **Description:** Integration, fundamental theorem of calculus, techniques, applications, Taylor polynomials.

## MAT 127 - Calculus C
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** C or higher in MAT 126
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 161 or MAT 132 or 142
- **Description:** Sequences, series, Taylor series, differential equations intro, parametric equations, polar coordinates.

## MAT 131 - Calculus I
- **Credits:** 4 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** B or higher in MAT 123 or level 5+ on math placement exam
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 151 or MAT 125 or 141
- **Description:** Limits, derivatives, applications. More rigorous than MAT 125/AMS 151.

## MAT 132 - Calculus II
- **Credits:** 4
- **Prereq:** C or higher in MAT 131 or AMS 151 or level 7+ on placement
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 161 or MAT 127 or 142
- **Description:** Integration, techniques, applications, sequences, series, Taylor series.

## MAT 141 - Analysis I
- **Credits:** 4 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** Level 5+ on math placement exam; honors or permission
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 151 or MAT 125 or 131
- **Description:** Honors calculus: rigorous treatment of limits, continuity, differentiation.

## MAT 200 - Logic, Language and Proof
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** MAT 127 or 132 or AMS 161 or level 9+ on placement
- **Description:** Mathematical reasoning and proof techniques: logic, sets, functions, relations, induction.

## MAT 203 - Calculus III with Applications
- **Credits:** 4
- **Prereq:** C or higher in AMS 161 or MAT 127 or 132 or 142
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 261
- **Description:** Vectors, partial derivatives, multiple integrals, line/surface integrals, Green's/Stokes' theorems.

## MAT 211 - Introduction to Linear Algebra
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** AMS 151 or MAT 125 or 131 or 141
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 210
- **Description:** Systems of equations, matrices, vector spaces, linear transformations, eigenvalues. Proof-oriented.

## MAT 250 - Introduction to Advanced Mathematics
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200 and (MAT 203 or AMS 261)
- **Description:** Bridge course to upper-division math. Proof techniques applied to analysis and algebra.

## MAT 303 - Calculus IV with Applications
- **Credits:** 4
- **Prereq:** MAT 203 or AMS 261
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 361
- **Description:** ODEs, systems of ODEs, Laplace transforms, series solutions, Fourier series.

## MAT 307 - Multivariable Calculus with Linear Algebra
- **Credits:** 4
- **Prereq:** MAT 132 or AMS 161; co-req MAT 200
- **Description:** Integrated course: linear algebra and multivariable calculus together.

## MAT 308 - Differential Equations with Linear Algebra
- **Credits:** 4
- **Prereq:** MAT 307 or (MAT 203 and MAT 211)
- **Description:** Integrated course: differential equations and advanced linear algebra.

## MAT 310 - Linear Algebra
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 211 and MAT 200
- **Description:** Advanced linear algebra: vector spaces, dual spaces, inner product spaces, canonical forms.

## MAT 311 - Number Theory
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200
- **Description:** Divisibility, primes, congruences, quadratic reciprocity, number-theoretic functions.

## MAT 312 - Applied Algebra
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** MAT 211 or AMS 210
- **Description:** Groups, rings, fields with applications to coding theory and cryptography. Similar to AMS 351.

## MAT 313 - Abstract Algebra
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 310 and MAT 200
- **Description:** Groups, rings, fields, Galois theory.

## MAT 314 - Abstract Algebra II
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 313
- **Description:** Continuation: modules, field extensions, Galois theory.

## MAT 315 - Advanced Linear Algebra
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 310
- **Description:** Tensor products, multilinear algebra, Jordan/rational canonical forms.

## MAT 319 - Foundations of Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200 and (MAT 203 or AMS 261)
- **Description:** Rigorous real analysis: sequences, series, continuity, differentiation, Riemann integration.

## MAT 320 - Introduction to Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 319
- **Description:** Continuation: metric spaces, compactness, connectedness, uniform convergence, function spaces.

## MAT 322 - Analysis in Several Dimensions
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 320
- **Description:** Differentiation in R^n, inverse/implicit function theorems, manifolds, differential forms.

## MAT 324 - Real Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 320
- **Description:** Lebesgue measure and integration, L^p spaces, Fourier analysis.

## MAT 331 - Computer-Assisted Mathematical Problem Solving
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200 or AMS 301 or CSE 114
- **Description:** Using Mathematica/Maple for mathematical exploration and problem solving.

## MAT 336 - History of Mathematics
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200
- **Description:** Historical development of mathematics from ancient to modern times.

## MAT 341 - Applied Real Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 203 or AMS 261; MAT 211 or AMS 210
- **Description:** Fourier series, orthogonal functions, boundary value problems, Sturm-Liouville theory.

## MAT 342 - Applied Complex Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 203 or AMS 261
- **Description:** Complex functions, analyticity, Cauchy's theorem, residues, conformal mapping, applications.

## MAT 344 - Complex Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 320
- **Description:** Rigorous complex analysis: analytic functions, Cauchy theory, residues, conformal mappings.

## MAT 351 - Differential Equations: Dynamics and Chaos
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 303 or AMS 361; MAT 200
- **Description:** Qualitative theory of ODEs, phase portraits, stability, bifurcation, chaos.

## MAT 360 - Geometric Structures
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200 and (MAT 211 or AMS 210)
- **Description:** Euclidean, affine, projective, and hyperbolic geometry from an axiomatic viewpoint.

## MAT 362 - Differential Geometry of Surfaces
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 203 or AMS 261; MAT 211 or AMS 210
- **Description:** Curves and surfaces in R^3, curvature, geodesics, Gauss-Bonnet theorem.

## MAT 364 - Topology and Geometry
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200
- **Description:** Surfaces, Euler characteristic, fundamental group, covering spaces, knot theory.

## MAT 371 - Logic
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200
- **Description:** Propositional/predicate logic, completeness, incompleteness, computability.

## MAT 373 - Analysis of Algorithms
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** MAT 200 or AMS 301 or CSE 214
- **Description:** Design and analysis of algorithms: sorting, searching, graph algorithms, NP-completeness.

## MAT 401 - Seminar in Mathematics
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** Permission of instructor
- **Description:** Advanced seminar on special topics. Content varies.

## MAT 475 - Undergraduate Teaching Practicum
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** EXP+
- **Prereq:** Permission of instructor
- **Description:** Supervised teaching experience in mathematics courses.

## MAT 487 - Independent Study in Special Topics
- **Credits:** 0-3
- **Prereq:** Permission of instructor
- **Description:** Independent study under faculty supervision.
