# AMS (Applied Mathematics and Statistics) Course Catalog

## AMS 102 - Elements of Statistics
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** Satisfaction of Skill 1 or QPS
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 110, 310, 311, 412; ECO 320; POL 201; PSY 201; SOC 202
- **Description:** Use and misuse of statistics; measures of central tendency and dispersion, probability, binomial/normal distributions, hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, chi square, regression.
- **Note:** Non-AMS majors only. HD/CA course.

## AMS 103 - Applied Mathematics in Modern Technology
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** TECH
- **Prereq:** None
- **Description:** Applications of math in modern technology: GPS, internet, digital music/images, cryptography, statistics.

## AMS 110 - Probability and Statistics in the Life Sciences
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** MAT 123 or level 3+ on math placement exam
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 102, 310, 311; ECO 320; POL 201; PSY 201; SOC 202
- **Description:** Probability and statistics for life sciences: probability, random variables, binomial/normal distributions, hypothesis testing, regression, ANOVA.

## AMS 151 - Applied Calculus I
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** B or higher in MAT 123, or level 5+ on math placement exam
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with MAT 125 or 131
- **Description:** Review of functions; differentiation methods, interpretations, applications; introduction to integration. For CEAS majors.
- **Note:** HD/CA course. Placement test level 9+ exempts this.

## AMS 161 - Applied Calculus II
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** QPS | **Grade:** A-C required
- **Prereq:** C or higher in AMS 151 or MAT 125 or 131, or level 7+ on math placement exam
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with MAT 127 or 132
- **Description:** Integration techniques, improper integrals, Taylor series, differential equations, multivariable calculus intro.
- **Note:** HD/CA course. Placement test level 9+ exempts this.

## AMS 210 - Applied Linear Algebra
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** AMS 151 or MAT 125 or 131 or 141, or level 7+ on math placement exam
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with MAT 211
- **Description:** Systems of linear equations, matrices, determinants, vector spaces, eigenvalues/eigenvectors. Applications to engineering/science.

## AMS 261 - Applied Calculus III
- **Credits:** 4 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** C or higher in AMS 161 or MAT 127 or 132 or 142
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with MAT 203
- **Description:** Vector algebra, multivariate differential and integral calculus, optimization, vector calculus (Green's, Stokes', divergence theorems).

## AMS 300 - Writing in Applied Mathematics
- **Credits:** 0-3 | **SBC:** WRTD
- **Prereq:** WRT 102, U3/U4 standing, AMS major
- **Description:** Writing course for AMS majors. May be fulfilled by WRTD-tagged AMS course.

## AMS 301 - Finite Mathematical Structures
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** AMS 210 or MAT 211; AMS 161 or MAT 127 or 132
- **Description:** Combinatorics, generating functions, recurrence relations, graph theory, network algorithms, matching theory.

## AMS 303 - Graph Theory
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 301
- **Description:** Graphs, trees, connectivity, Euler/Hamilton paths, coloring, planarity, network flows, matching.

## AMS 310 - Survey of Probability and Statistics
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** AMS 161 or MAT 127 or 132 or 142
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with AMS 311
- **Description:** Probability, random variables, distributions, expectation, estimation, hypothesis testing, regression, ANOVA.

## AMS 311 - Probability Theory
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** AMS 261 or MAT 203
- **Description:** Probability spaces, conditional probability, random variables, distributions, expectation, generating functions, limit theorems.

## AMS 315 - Data Analysis
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** CER, ESI
- **Prereq:** AMS 210 or MAT 211; AMS 310 or 311
- **Description:** Statistical computing, data visualization, inference methods using R. Exploratory data analysis, regression, model selection.

## AMS 316 - Introduction to Time Series Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 315 or 572
- **Description:** Time series models (AR, MA, ARMA, ARIMA), spectral analysis, forecasting.

## AMS 317 - Introduction to Linear Regression Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 210 or MAT 211; AMS 310 or 311
- **Description:** Simple/multiple linear regression, model diagnostics, variable selection, residual analysis.

## AMS 318 - Financial Mathematics
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** WRTD
- **Prereq:** AMS 161 or MAT 127 or 132; AMS 310 or 311
- **Description:** Interest theory, annuities, bonds, financial derivatives, Black-Scholes model, portfolio optimization.

## AMS 320 - Introduction to Quantitative Finance
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 310 or 311; AMS 210 or MAT 211
- **Description:** Financial markets, asset pricing, derivatives, risk management, portfolio theory.

## AMS 325 - Computing and Programming Fundamentals
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 210 or MAT 211
- **Description:** Programming fundamentals for AMS: data structures, algorithms, numerical methods. Python/MATLAB.

## AMS 326 - Numerical Analysis
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 210 or MAT 211; AMS 261 or MAT 203; programming course
- **Description:** Numerical solutions of equations, interpolation, numerical differentiation/integration, ODEs.

## AMS 332 - Computational Modeling of Physiological Systems
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 361 or MAT 303
- **Description:** Mathematical modeling of biological systems: neural, cardiac, metabolic systems.

## AMS 333 - Mathematical Biology
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 361 or MAT 303
- **Description:** Mathematical models in biology: population dynamics, epidemiology, ecology, genetics.

## AMS 335 - Game Theory
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 301 or 310
- **Description:** Strategic games, Nash equilibrium, cooperative games, auctions, mechanism design.

## AMS 341 - Operations Research I: Deterministic Models
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 210 or MAT 211
- **Description:** Linear programming, simplex method, duality, sensitivity analysis, integer programming, network flows.

## AMS 342 - Operations Research II: Stochastic Models
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 310 or 311; AMS 341
- **Description:** Markov chains, queueing theory, inventory models, simulation, decision analysis.

## AMS 345 - Computational Geometry
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 301; CSE 214 or programming course
- **Description:** Convex hulls, Voronoi diagrams, triangulations, geometric searching, motion planning.

## AMS 351 - Applied Algebra
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** AMS 210 or MAT 211
- **Description:** Groups, rings, fields with applications to coding theory, cryptography, symmetry.

## AMS 361 - Applied Calculus IV: Differential Equations
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** STEM+
- **Prereq:** AMS 261 or MAT 203
- **Anti-req:** Not for credit with MAT 303
- **Description:** First/second order ODEs, systems, Laplace transforms, series solutions, boundary value problems.

## AMS 380 - Data Mining
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 315 or equivalent
- **Description:** Classification, clustering, association rules, dimensionality reduction, text mining, deep learning basics.

## AMS 394 - Statistical Laboratory
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** CER, ESI, EXP+
- **Prereq:** AMS 315 or 572
- **Description:** Hands-on statistical consulting. Students work on real data analysis projects.

## AMS 412 - Mathematical Statistics
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 311; AMS 315 or 361
- **Description:** Sampling distributions, point/interval estimation, hypothesis testing, Bayesian methods.

## AMS 441 - Business Enterprise
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** U4 standing, AMS major
- **Description:** Business concepts for AMS students: financial statements, marketing, operations.

## AMS 475 - Undergraduate Teaching Practicum
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** EXP+, SPK
- **Prereq:** U3/U4 standing, permission of instructor
- **Description:** Supervised teaching in AMS courses. May be repeated once.
- **Note:** Limited elective (max 2 from 475/476/487)

## AMS 476 - Undergraduate Teaching Practicum
- **Credits:** 3 | **SBC:** EXP+, SPK
- **Prereq:** AMS 475
- **Description:** Continuation of AMS 475.
- **Note:** Limited elective (max 2 from 475/476/487)

## AMS 487 - Research in Applied Mathematics and Statistics
- **Credits:** 0-3 | **SBC:** EXP+, WRTD
- **Prereq:** Permission of instructor
- **Description:** Independent research under faculty supervision. May be repeated.
- **Note:** Limited elective (max 2 from 475/476/487)

## AMS 488 - Internship in Applied Mathematics and Statistics
- **Credits:** 0-3 | **SBC:** EXP+, WRTD
- **Prereq:** Permission of instructor
- **Description:** Supervised internship in industry or government.

## AMS 492 - Topics in Applied Mathematics
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** Permission of instructor
- **Description:** Special topics course. Content varies by semester.

## Graduate courses commonly taken by undergrads at SUNY Korea

## AMS 507 - Introduction to Probability
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 311 or equivalent
- **Description:** Measure-theoretic probability, convergence, characteristic functions, central limit theorem.

## AMS 510 - Analytical Methods for AMS
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 261, AMS 210
- **Description:** Complex analysis, transform methods, asymptotic analysis, special functions.

## AMS 540 - Linear Programming
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 210 or MAT 211
- **Description:** Simplex method, duality, sensitivity, interior point methods, applications.

## AMS 570 - Introduction to Mathematical Statistics
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 311 or equivalent
- **Description:** Sampling theory, estimation, hypothesis testing, sufficiency, completeness.

## AMS 572 - Data Analysis I
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 570 or 310
- **Description:** Applied regression, ANOVA, nonparametric methods, categorical data analysis using SAS/R.

## AMS 578 - Regression Theory
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 572
- **Description:** Linear models theory, generalized least squares, diagnostics, model selection.

## AMS 580 - Statistical Learning
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 572 or equivalent
- **Description:** Supervised/unsupervised learning, regularization, trees, SVM, neural networks.

## AMS 597 - Statistical Computing
- **Credits:** 3
- **Prereq:** AMS 572 or equivalent
- **Description:** Computational statistics, EM algorithm, MCMC, bootstrap, simulation methods.
